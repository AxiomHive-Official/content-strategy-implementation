**Challenge 1: Limited Founder Time**
- **Mitigation:** Batch content creation (4 hours monthly = 8-12 posts)
- **Contingency:** Hire ghostwriter familiar with technical AI content

**Challenge 2: Low Initial Engagement**
- **Mitigation:** Leverage paid amplification for first 30 days
- **Contingency:** Pivot content pillars based on early feedback

**Challenge 3: Competitive Noise**
- **Mitigation:** Focus on deterministic AI niche, own the narrative
- **Contingency:** Develop proprietary data/research for differentiation

**Challenge 4: Measuring ROI**
- **Mitigation:** Implement UTM tracking and attribution from day 1
- **Contingency:** Track qualitative indicators (meeting quality, brand mentions)
